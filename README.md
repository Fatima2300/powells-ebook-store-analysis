# Customer Spending & Subscription Analysis  
Powell’s eBook Store – Tableau Project

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer spending behavior and subscription engagement using Tableau to identify high-value customer segments and support data-driven marketing decisions.
The primary objective of this analysis is to answer:
What customer segments generate the highest revenue and subscription activity?

---

## Executive Summary

The Executive Dashboard provides a high-level business overview using the following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
- Average Monthly Spend  
- Total Revenue  
- Total Customers  
- Subscription Rate (%)  

These KPIs provide a quick assessment of overall business performance and customer engagement.

---

## Key Insights

- Management and Professional occupations generate the highest average monthly spending.
- Customers with higher education levels tend to spend more per month.
- Subscription engagement varies by gender, indicating potential segmentation opportunities.
- Geographic spending variation across states is moderate.
- Certain cities demonstrate stronger subscription activity.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Focus premium marketing efforts on high-spending occupational segments.
- Develop targeted subscription campaigns for underperforming demographic groups.
- Use education level as a segmentation variable for personalized marketing.
- Implement localized campaigns in high-engagement cities.
